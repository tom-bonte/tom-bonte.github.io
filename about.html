Beyond the Resume

A look into my journey, passions, and life outside of work.

🌍 The Journey So Far

My career has been anything but a straight line. Originally from Belgium, I started my academic journey deep in the exact sciences, studying Civil and Industrial Engineering at KU Leuven. While I loved the analytical problem-solving required in engineering, I quickly realized my true passion lay in dynamic, global environments.

That realization led me to swap the traditional office for the world map. Over the past decade, I have lived, worked, and managed complex operations across Mexico, Egypt, Costa Rica, and now Spain. Adapting to diverse cultures, learning new languages on the fly, and solving high-stress logistical puzzles has shaped not just my professional career, but my entire worldview.

🏠 Life at Home & Wellness

While my background is rooted in fast-paced tourism and operations, life at home in Cabo de Palos is much more grounded. I share my daily routine with my cat, Nori. Over the past year, I have taken major steps to prioritize my long-term health and well-being, and I am proud to have been completely smoke-free since October 2024.
