Beyond the Resume

A look into my journey, passions, and life outside of work.

🌍 The Journey So Far

Growing up in Belgium, about an hour from the coast, I developed an early fascination with the brute force of the ocean and spent family trips exploring tide pools. That curiosity was nurtured by the digital encyclopedias on my family's old Windows 98 PC. While I initially followed an academic path into Civil and Industrial Engineering, a coincidental introduction dive during a trip to Turkey changed my trajectory entirely. I was hooked.

My global journey officially began in 2012 with a marine conservation program in Cambodia. It was my first real culture shock and taste of profound freedom. In 2013, I headed to Playa del Carmen, Mexico, for an intensive internship. That's where I truly became a professional, guiding dives and discovering a hidden talent: languages. Leveraging my Belgian background, I quickly became the go-to translator for guests. Hearing Brazilian Portuguese on the resort beach inspired me so much that upon returning home, I launched a 100-day mission to teach myself the language, adding a sixth language to my skill set.

Ready for the next challenge, I moved to Hurghada, Egypt. Between 2015 and 2016, I matured from a rookie into an IDC Staff Instructor with over 1,200 dives, learning that true leadership means adapting your approach to the person in front of you. After a stint back in Belgium navigating the rigors of cold-water drysuit diving and managing independent operations, the tropics called me back. I moved to Costa Rica in 2018, where I managed Rich Coast Diving under two Course Directors, ultimately achieving my Master Instructor rating and surpassing 2,500 dives. Today, I bring that same adaptability and operational focus to new challenges from my base in Spain.

🏠 Life at Home & Wellness

While my background is rooted in fast-paced international operations, life at home in Cabo de Palos is much more grounded. I share my daily routine with my Venezuelan wife, Desi, and our cat, Nori. Over the past year, I have taken major steps to prioritize my long-term health and well-being, and I am incredibly proud to have been completely smoke-free since October 2024.
